About the System

The Farmer-Buyer Direct Market Management System is designed to eliminate intermediaries and create a direct connection between farmers and buyers. This platform ensures fair pricing, timely payments, and transparent transactions. Farmers can list their crops with quantities and prices, while buyers can search for required crops and connect directly with farmers.
